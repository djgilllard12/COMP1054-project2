Dualshock 4 Controller

Product by: Sony Interactive Entertainment

Released on: November 15 2013

Price: $69.99

In stock

The Dualshock 4 is the all new controller for the Playstation 4. We have used player feedback to retool the and modify the controller over the years to give our users the best experience.

We've added a number of new features to this redition of the classic Dualshock controller. A built in speaker will allow games to output audio through both your tv and controller. We've also added a share button that will allow you to instantly save and upload images to your favourites social medias. And finally we've added a touch pad that will allow for navigation options.

The Dualshock 4 comes in Black, Blue, Silver and it charges via micro USB cable.
